Elite 10 Quant System
Step 1: The Daily "Elite 10" Selection
Every morning (or every 15 days for stability), calculate the relationship between all Nifty 50 stocks and the Index using the last 30 trading days of 5-minute data.
Calculate Returns:
"Return"=(〖"Price" 〗_t-〖"Price" 〗_(t-1))/〖"Price" 〗_(t-1) 
for both the Stock and the Nifty 50 Index.
Calculate Correlation (r): The Pearson correlation coefficient between Stock returns and Index returns. Target the lowest values (independence).
Calculate Beta (β):
β="Cov(Stock,Index)" /"Var(Index)" 
Target the highest values (volatility).
Ranking: Rank stocks by Beta (highest to lowest) and Correlation (lowest to highest). The Elite 10 are the stocks with the best combined rank.
Step 2: 09:20 AM Continuation Sniper
Goal: Capture the most aggressive directional move of the day.
Morning Thresholds:
	Intraday Relative Strength (IRS): >0.45%
	Relative Volume (RelVol): >1.3x
	Stock Gap: |Gap|<0.80%
The Alignment Rule:
	Primary: "Sign (Stock Gap)"=="Sign (IRS Direction)" 
	Index Guard: "Sign (Index Gap)"≠-"Sign (IRS Direction)"  (The Index must not be gapping against your trade)


Execution:
	Entry: Market order at 09:20:01 AM
	Stop Loss (SL): Low of the 9:15 candle (for Longs) or High of the 9:15 candle (for Shorts)
	Take Profit (TP): 2.0% (utilizing the high max potential of these stocks)
	Trailing: Move SL to Breakeven once the profit hits +1.0%
Step 3: 11:30 AM Reversal Income
Goal: Trade the mean-reversion of an overextended stock.
Exhaustion Thresholds:
	Eligibility: No Continuation trade was taken on this stock today
	Gap Requirement: |Gap|>0.50%
	ADR Coverage: >80%
	IRS: > 1.0%
The Trigger:
	IRS Flattening: |IRS_(11:30) |<|IRS_(11:00) | (Verification that momentum has peaked)
Execution:
	Direction: Opposite to the morning trend
	Stop Loss (SL): Day's High/Low ±0.3%
	Take Profit (TP): 1.0%

# Essential Risk Rules
	One Trade Per Stock: Never trade the same symbol twice in one day. Continuation always takes priority over Reversal.
	Capital Allocation: Divide capital equally across any stocks that trigger simultaneously.
	The 03:15 Rule: All positions, regardless of profit or loss, MUST be squared off by 03:15 PM. No exceptions.
	No Trade Zone: If the Nifty 50 Index has a gap >1.0%, skip all Continuation trades as the "Index Gravity" is too high.
